Curious Forest Instructions

The game is easy!

  • An image will load with a text prompt and three buttons.
  • Each button will result in an action, which could result in you losing or gaining health points.
  • You select your choice, and the game will move on. It will adjust your health accordingly and you will proceed to the next level.
  • After a series of levels, you will face a final Boss. Beat that and you've beaten the game!
  • If you want to return to the beginning and restart, just click the icon next to the game title, and that will take you back to the beginning.

Have you read the instructions?

All you need to do is type 'yes' in if you've read the instructions and you can play the game
